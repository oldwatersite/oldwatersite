Water Transfer Printing (Hydro Dipping)

Water transfer printing is a decorative process for complex and curved surfaces. Printed film patterns are transferred evenly onto product surfaces.

We provide a wide pattern library, including walnut wood, camouflage, carbon style, marble, and more. Custom pattern development is also available.

Common Use Cases

Typical applications include dashboard trims, motorcycle fairings, furniture parts, decorative accessories, and consumer product housings.

Hydro dipping supports plastic, metal, ceramic, and glass substrates with excellent visual consistency.
